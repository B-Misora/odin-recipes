Lasagna

Lasagna a dish that originates from Italy but has stolen the hearts of all. A regular staple in any home and a childrens faviourite. I mean it's cheese paste and meat most of the favourites stacked in one dish!!!

To find out how to make a lovely timeless favourite please continue reading!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Grarlic
  • Pasata
  • Sugar
  • Italian herbs and spices blend
  • Lasanga pasta sheets
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Bechamel

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the pasta sheets
  3. Make the Bechamel sauce
  4. Layer the lasanga with in this order: Meat sauce, pasta sheets, bechemel, cheese. Keep going ensuring chease is at the top
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
